About Kamay The Premium

A destination where luxury, comfort, and hospitality come together to create unforgettable experiences in Gurugram.

Luxury & Comfort Redefined

Located in the heart of Gurugram, Kamay The Premium offers a perfect blend of elegance, comfort, and affordability. Designed for both business and leisure travelers, the hotel provides a premium stay experience with warm hospitality and modern amenities.

With 30 thoughtfully designed rooms, stylish interiors, and exceptional guest services, Kamay The Premium ensures every visitor enjoys a peaceful, relaxing, and memorable stay.

Our commitment to quality service, personalized attention, and modern comfort makes us an ideal destination for corporate stays, family vacations, and special occasions.
